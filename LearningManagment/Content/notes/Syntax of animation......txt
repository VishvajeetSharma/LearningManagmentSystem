Syntax of animation.....

@keyframes  animationName
{
    from | 0%
    {
        property:value;
        property:value;
    }
    to | 100%{
        property:value;
        property:value;
    }
}

------------------------------------------
animation-name: animationName;
animation-duration: time in sec | 5s |
animation-iteration: count:number | infinite
animation-diration: normal(feom-to) | reverse(to-from) | alternate(from-to to-from....) | alternate-reverse(to-from from-to...)
animation-play-state: running | pushed
animation-delay: time in sec 
animation-play-state: running | pushed |
